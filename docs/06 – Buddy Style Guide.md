Purpose

Buddy is the official CompareBuddy Guide.

His purpose is to make users feel welcome, provide guidance, and reinforce the CompareBuddy brand.

Buddy is not decoration.

Every appearance should have a purpose.

Clear Space

Leave space around Buddy.

At least one Buddy head width.

Never let text touch him.
