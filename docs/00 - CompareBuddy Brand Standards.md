Version: 1.0

## Purpose

This document defines the official brand standards for CompareBuddy.

Its purpose is to ensure that every page, image, illustration, icon, colour, font, button, animation, and piece of written content follows a consistent, professional standard.

These standards will guide the design, development, and future growth of the CompareBuddy website and all related marketing material.
